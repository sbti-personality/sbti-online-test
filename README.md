## 📖 A Story of "Redemption" Hidden in a Prank
The origins of SBTI are rooted in a story that is as "savage" as it is heartwarming.
Video creator "Apu" had a friend who loved drinking so much he would carry baijiu in a thermos even during work hours
. Determined to help him quit, Apu painstakingly built this "bootleg" personality test as a deep intervention
. When the friend finished the test, he was met with a pre-set "alcoholic personality" result, complete with warnings about cancer risks and health dangers
.
The result? The friend pledged to quit drinking the very next day
.
The core of SBTI is actually the deepest form of care between friends. It is more than just a set of questions; it is a gentle, humorous way to intervene in the lives of those we care about
.
## 🧪 How Does It Work?
Despite its "parody" label, SBTI is surprisingly comprehensive in its content:
15 Assessment Dimensions: It covers everything from life philosophy and values to social styles and action drives
.
30+ Curated Questions: It takes only 5–10 minutes to complete a deep dialogue with your inner self
.
27 Unique Personalities: Every type comes with a dedicated analysis
. You might discover you are the rare CTRL (The Controller), or perhaps the HHHH (The Laughing Fool)—a special fallback type assigned only when your answers don't match any standard category
.
Hidden Surprises: True to its roots, the test includes hidden alcohol-related questions
. If you provide a very specific combination of answers, you might unlock the secret DRUNK type
.
